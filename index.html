Hello!

Since my early years of high-school, I evolved a passion for problem solving, design and innovation. I merged myself into the world of engineering and decided to pursue my undergraduate degree in Civil Engineering. The idea of designing a physical item that influenced everyday life thrilled me - I loved knowing that I contributed, even in a small way.

This passion led to more, and in every internship I had, I saw myself doing more than just engineering. It was me who would spot an issue and initiated some sort of project that could facilitate the work of others by creating solutions through spreadsheets - having my first tastes of empathy.

I noticed that coming to a solution and seeing it positively impact people’s life in a much more direct way was even more exciting - And so, I pivoted ...

My ongoing strive to learn, has brought me on the path to creating beautiful and intuitive products. Today, I continue strengthening my skills in:

  • Project/Product Management
  • User Experience
  • Front-end Development
  • Quality Assurance

Read about some of my recent projects or contact me for more information.

When I am not pressing keys, talking to clients and managing priorities, I am DIYing a project, baking, practicing Yoga, enjoying a cold IPA or a good cup (or 2) of coffee.
